## 1. Introduction

FitSphere is a smart gym management system designed to simplify gym operations while improving the overall fitness experience for members. The system enables administrators to manage memberships, trainers, workout plans, diet plans, attendance, payments, and reports from a centralized dashboard. Members can access personalized workout routines, diet plans, progress tracking, and class bookings through both a web application and an Android application.

The project aims to replace traditional manual record keeping with a modern digital platform that improves efficiency, accuracy, and user experience.

---

## 2. Objectives

• Digitize gym management.

• Reduce paperwork.

• Improve communication between trainers and members.

• Provide personalized workout plans.

• Track fitness progress.

• Manage memberships and attendance.

• Generate reports.

• Provide a responsive web application and Android application.

---

## 3. Scope

The system serves three types of users:

• Administrator

• Trainer

• Member

The project provides complete gym management including authentication, membership handling, attendance, workout management, diet planning, payment records, notifications, and progress tracking.

Future versions may include AI-powered workout recommendations, smartwatch integration, and nutrition analysis.

---

## 4. User Requirements

### 4.1 Administrator

The administrator is responsible for managing the entire gym management system.

Functions:

- Login securely
- Manage member accounts
- Manage trainer accounts
- Create membership plans
- Assign trainers
- Manage attendance
- Manage workout templates
- Manage diet templates
- View payment history
- Generate reports
- Send announcements

---

### 4.2 Trainer

The trainer manages assigned members and monitors their fitness progress.

Functions:

- Login securely
- View assigned members
- Upload workout plans
- Update diet plans
- Monitor member progress
- Record attendance
- Send notifications

---

### 4.3 Member

Members use the system to manage their fitness journey.

Functions:

- Register/Login
- Purchase membership
- Renew membership
- View workout plans
- View diet plans
- Track body measurements
- View progress charts
- Book training sessions
- View payment history
- Receive notifications
